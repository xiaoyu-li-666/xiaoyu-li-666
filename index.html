Selected Research

A few themes that define my recent work.

Older Adults and Thermal Comfort

Health · Biometeorology · Environmental exposure

Developing age-appropriate thermal comfort models and evaluating heat exposure in daily living environments.

LiDAR and 3D Urban Morphology

Geospatial analytics · Urban form · Fine-scale assessment

Linking three-dimensional green space and urban form characteristics to microscale thermal conditions.

Landscape Design for Heat Mitigation

Evidence-based design · Urban resilience

Translating climate measurements and simulation into practical landscape strategies for cooler cities.

Digital Twin and Human-Centered Urban Science

Modeling · Public health · Urban analytics

Combining environmental modeling, behavioral data, and machine learning to support healthier urban environments.

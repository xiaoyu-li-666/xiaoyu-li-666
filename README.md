## 🔬 About

Xiaoyu Li is a Ph.D. Candidate (ABD) at Texas A&M University, specializing in **urban climate, environmental health, and landscape architecture**.

Her research focuses on how **outdoor environments affect thermal exposure, comfort, and well-being**, especially for **older adults and heat-vulnerable populations**.

Her work integrates:

* Urban microclimate modeling
* Environmental exposure analysis
* Landscape performance
* Public health and aging
* Geospatial analytics and simulation

---

## 🌡️ Research Areas

* **Urban Microclimate & Thermal Comfort**
  Modeling heat exposure and pedestrian comfort across cities

* **Healthy Aging & Heat Vulnerability**
  Designing environments for aging populations under climate stress

* **Climate-Responsive Landscape Design**
  Data-driven strategies for resilient urban environments

* **Digital Twins & Environmental Modeling**
  Integrating LiDAR, GIS, and simulation into design workflows
